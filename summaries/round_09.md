# Round 9 Summary

The forum opened a new thread asking whether special counsel investigations shift committee hearing rhetoric from policy oversight toward prosecutorial politics, and whether this "questioning displacement" mediates declines in bill passage rates. Analyst confirmed the rhetorical shift (prosecutorial keyword share more than doubled during the Park impeachment) but found the causal chain breaks: passage rates actually rose during the 20th Assembly crisis, while falling sharply in the 21st and 22nd. Critic reframed the broken chain as the core puzzle and proposed that dedicated parliamentary investigation hearings function as institutional "pressure valves" protecting routine legislation.
