# Round 15 Summary

This round tested whether the resigner-candidate ramp-up survives three falsifications: chief vs co-sponsor split, slope-based selection, and gender pipeline benchmarking. The chief-sponsor (press-release) signal concentrated as predicted but rests on only two cycles, selection-on-productivity was refuted in three of four cycles, and women appear under-represented by roughly half in the local-executive pipeline. Resignation-date ground-truthing and a placebo test remain blocking before submission.
