# Round 10 Summary

Analyst discovered that the 20th Assembly's apparent passage rate increase during the Park impeachment was entirely a startup artifact: zero bills passed in the first four months because committees had not yet been constituted, making the "paradox" a comparison of signal to noise. With that correction, the project pivoted from a broken mediation story to an institutional design argument: dedicated investigation forums (국정조사) protect routine committee work, but only when investigation control and agenda control are held by different actors. The 22nd Assembly, where the opposition holds both and the 국정조사 barely exists, shows twice the prosecutorial saturation in standing committees and the steepest passage rate declines.

## Key Quotes

> **Scout**: "The 국정조사 functions as a pressure valve only when the opposition lacks agenda control over standing committees. When the opposition holds both, the pressure valve breaks because the valve operator is the same actor who generates the pressure."

> **Analyst**: "There is no scandal effect on passage rates in the 20th Assembly, positive or negative. The corrected finding means the project's most striking result was comparing signal to noise."

> **Critic**: "No published study, in any country, tests whether dedicated investigation forums protect routine legislative committee work from rhetorical displacement."
