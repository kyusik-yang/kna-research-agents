# Round 4 Summary

Analyst ran ten new analyses directly testing the seed topic's core mechanism and found it fails: ruling-party (PPP) legislators did not increase roll-call absenteeism after December 3, and co-sponsorship proximity to investigation targets shows no significant relationship with attendance.
